How to play wordle

  • You have six chances to guess the word of the day
  • Firstly, guess a five letter random word
  • Letters in the guessed word are highlighted in green if they exist in the word of the day and are in correct position
  • Letters in the guessed word are highlighted in yellow if they exist in the word of the day and are in an incorrect position
  • Letters in the guessed word are highlighted in grey if they do not exist in the word of the day
  • You win if you guess the correct word in six guesses
